About Us

Helping Hands NGO was founded in 2010 to support underprivileged communities.

We believe in sustainable development through education and healthcare.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and families in need by providing them with access to resources, opportunities, and a network of support. We believe that small acts of kindness can create lasting change.

What We Do

  • 📚 Offer free education and tutoring for children
  • 🩺 Organize health camps and awareness programs
  • 🍲 Distribute food and essential supplies in rural areas
  • 👩‍💻 Conduct skill development workshops for youth and women

Our Impact

Over the last decade, Helping Hands has reached more than 50,000 lives across villages and urban slums. Our dedicated team of volunteers works year-round to respond to emergencies, educate communities, and build a better tomorrow.

Join Us

Whether you're looking to volunteer, donate, or partner with us — your support helps us grow stronger and reach farther. Together, we can bring hope and dignity to every hand we touch.
